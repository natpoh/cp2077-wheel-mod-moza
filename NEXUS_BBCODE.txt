[size=5][b]Cyberpunk 2077 — Moza & Direct Drive Wheel Support[/b][/size]

[b]v2.31.9[/b] for Cyberpunk 2077 game patch 2.31.

Full racing wheel support for Cyberpunk 2077. Drive with a [b]Moza[/b] or any other [b]Direct Drive[/b] steering wheel! Your wheel handles steering, throttle, brake, and clutch. Force feedback fires from in-game physics (centering as you turn, cornering load, surface texture, collision jolts, slip when you lose grip). 

There is no virtual gamepad to install, no XInput shim, no third-party driver. The mod hooks the game directly via DirectInput/Logitech SDK.

[size=4][b]Supported wheels[/b][/size]
The mod was designed to work with DirectInput-compatible wheels. Tested and confirmed on:

[b]Moza Racing & Others:[/b]
[list]
[*]Moza R5
[*]Moza R3
[*]Fanatec / Thrustmaster (requires manual mapping)
[/list]
[b]How to map axes for non-Logitech wheels:[/b]
Download [url=https://github.com/natpoh/cp2077-wheel-mod-moza/raw/main/tools/input_probe/input_probe.exe][b]input_probe.exe[/b][/url] from GitHub and run it. It will show you the exact names of your steering, throttle, and brake axes. Open [b]red4ext\plugins\direct_wheel\config.json[/b] and type those names into the "axes" section!

[b]Logitech:[/b]
[list]
[*]G923 (Xbox, PS, PC variants)
[*]G920, G29, G27, G25
[*]Momo Force, Driving Force GT
[/list]
The mod auto-detects what hardware your Logitech wheel actually has (force feedback motor, rev-strip LEDs, lower-cluster buttons) and only shows the relevant settings.

[size=4][b]New Features[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]Custom Steering Equalizer:[/b] 3-point equalizer (25%, 50%, 75%) to customize your steering curve and perfectly counteract the game's internal exponential steering physics!
[*][b]Speed Steering Boost:[/b] Compensates steering at speed (up to 3x multiplier) so you can still turn at 200 mph.
[/list]

[size=4][b]Install[/b][/size]
[list=1]
[*]Click the [b]Vortex[/b] download button on this page.
[*]Vortex will ask about a file conflict on mod_settings.dll. Choose this mod to win the conflict (load after Mod Settings). The included file is a small patch on top of upstream Mod Settings.
[/list]
[i](Manual Download is also available if you don't use Vortex, just extract the zip directly into your Cyberpunk 2077 folder and overwrite when asked).[/i]

[size=4][b]Configuration / Setup[/b][/size]
It is highly recommended to set your wheel's rotation angle / operating range to [b]720 degrees[/b] in your wheel software (Moza Pit House, Logitech G HUB, Fanatec Control Panel, etc.) for the best steering response.

[size=4][b]Troubleshooting[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]Wheel detected but the car doesn't move.[/b] Make sure RED4ext is installed and current.
[*][b]Settings page is missing.[/b] Mod Settings or ArchiveXL aren't installed correctly.
[*][b]Three orphan toggles in settings.[/b] Vortex installed stock Mod Settings on top of the patched build that ships with this mod. Re-deploy in Vortex and let this mod win the file conflict.
[/list]
