[size=5][b]Cyberpunk 2077 — Moza & Direct Drive Wheel Support[/b][/size]

[b]v3.0.1[/b] for Cyberpunk 2077 game patch 2.31.

Full racing wheel support for Cyberpunk 2077. Drive with a [b]Moza[/b] or any other [b]Direct Drive[/b] steering wheel! Your wheel handles steering, throttle, brake, and clutch. Force feedback fires from in-game physics (centering as you turn, cornering load, surface texture, collision jolts, slip when you lose grip). 

There is no virtual gamepad to install, no XInput shim, no third-party driver. The mod hooks the game directly via DirectInput/Logitech SDK.

[size=4][b]Supported wheels[/b][/size]
The mod was designed to work with DirectInput-compatible wheels. Tested and confirmed on:

[b]Moza Racing & Others:[/b]
[list]
[*]Moza R5
[*]Moza R3
[*]Fanatec / Thrustmaster (requires manual mapping)
[/list]
[b]How to map axes for non-Logitech wheels:[/b]
Go to [b]Mod Settings → G-series Wheel → Axis mapping[/b] and select the correct throttle/brake axes from the dropdown. To identify your axes, download [url=https://github.com/natpoh/cp2077-wheel-mod-moza/raw/main/tools/input_probe.exe][b]input_probe.exe[/b][/url] from GitHub — it shows all connected devices with their axis values in real time.

[b]Logitech:[/b]
[list]
[*]G923 (Xbox, PS, PC variants)
[*]G920, G29, G27, G25
[*]Momo Force, Driving Force GT
[/list]
The mod auto-detects what hardware your Logitech wheel actually has (force feedback motor, rev-strip LEDs, lower-cluster buttons) and only shows the relevant settings.

[size=4][b]New Features[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]Automatic Steering Linearization:[/b] The game applies a quadratic curve to steering (actual_steer ≈ input²), which kills small-angle precision — at 10° wheel turn you only get 3.6% of the expected response. The mod applies the mathematical inverse (√x) before the game processes it, so √x → game squares it → x. Your steering is perfectly linear across the full wheel range, no manual tuning needed.
[*][b]Speed Steering Boost:[/b] Compensates steering at speed by amplifying the linearized signal up to a 2x multiplier at 100 mph, so you can still turn perfectly at high speeds.
[*][b]Steering Speed Configurator:[/b] It is [b]highly recommended[/b] to also install our standalone [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/cyberpunk2077/mods/29868]Steering Speed Configurator[/url] mod. It allows you to push the engine's steering physical limits (steering turn speed and re-center speed). For wheel play, setting both to 6+ in that mod gives accurate and fast steering responsiveness!
[*][b]Invert Pedals:[/b] Separate settings to invert throttle and brake pedals directly in the mod menu.
[*][b]Device & Axis Mapping:[/b] Select wheel and pedal devices independently (for split-device USB setups) and pick throttle/brake axes from an in-game dropdown — no more editing config.json!
[/list]

[size=4][b]Configuration / Setup[/b][/size]
It is highly recommended to set your wheel's rotation angle / operating range to [b]720 degrees[/b] in your wheel software (Moza Pit House, Logitech G HUB, Fanatec Control Panel, etc.) for the best steering response.

[size=4][b]Troubleshooting[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]Wheel detected but the car doesn't move.[/b] Make sure RED4ext is installed and current.
[*][b]Settings page is missing.[/b] Mod Settings or ArchiveXL aren't installed correctly.
[/list]
